Research

The AESS Research Section at Jordan University of Science and Technology focuses on exploring advanced topics in aerospace and electronic systems. We support student-led research, provide guidance on academic projects, and help members develop strong scientific and technical skills. Our goal is to inspire innovation, encourage critical thinking, and contribute to impactful research within the AESS and wider IEEE community.
